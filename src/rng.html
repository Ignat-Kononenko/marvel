Mission

We believe resilience and reliability are the key pillars of our energy future. As such, our work is dedicated to driving practical solutions that bolster energy security and economic and societal value.

Marvel Power Group structures large-scale commodity transactions with a dedicated focus on renewable natural gas (RNG), renewable thermal credits (RTCs) and other environmental and efficiency attributes for companies committed to market-driven, sustainable outcomes.

Our principals are experienced practitioners who have run companies, originated and closed complex deals, advocated and negotiated favorable policy and advanced technological innovation. With nearly four decades of commercial experience in energy, our customers chose us, every time, for our unparalleled industry knowledge, market acumen, professionalism and commitment to their prosperity.

Their mandates are complex: balance project needs with customer demands, regulatory frameworks, lofty investment targets and developmental complexities. Layer on market volatility and regulatory risk and the scope becomes less manageable in-house. This is where Marvel comes in. We understand the task and bring deep expertise to addressing these challenges.
