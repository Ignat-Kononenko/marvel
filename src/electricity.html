Mission

Marvel converts regulatory complexity and grid dynamics into practical, commercially-oriented strategies.

We support market participants with navigating regulations and executing power infrastructure projects. We specialize in translating policy directives into deal strategy that captures substantial, often-missed value.

As the industry's only independent advisor with in-house experience across utilities (PG&E), hyperscalers (Google), grid-tech (AMS, EnerNOC) and global energy leaders (Shell, BP, Pattern), Marvel pairs regulatory depth with commercial sophistication. This enables us to uncover hidden value, anticipate friction points, and design solutions that work across diverse market environments.

Services

Market Intelligence

Report: An investor-grade brief covering commercial activity in the data center-utility space, with a deal-by-deal view of large-load transactions across U.S. jurisdictions. The brief examines utility tariff structures, large-load contracting pathways (e.g., ESAs), generation interconnection and physical/financial responsibility configurations, risk-allocation mapping, and regulatory stakeholder dynamics shaping time-to-power and overall project economics.

Regulatory and
Commercial Advisory

Retainer: Ongoing, forward-looking monitoring of regulatory proceedings and market developments, with direct translation into commercial strategy, deal implications, capital impact, and competitive positioning. Engagements structured as continuous support or "sprints".

Expertise

Marvel is the only regulatory advisor that brings firsthand commercial experience from every seat at the decision table: utility negotiator, hyperscale load, independent power producer, technology expert and regulatory strategist.

We understand the incentives, constraints, and approval pressures each party faces because we’ve operated inside these organizations, built their business cases, negotiated their agreements, and navigated their regulatory processes. This multidimensional perspective allows us to translate policy, tariff design, and interconnection realities into practical guidance that reflects how decisions are actually made.

Our work is grounded in commercial realism, not theory, and is designed to help clients anticipate issues, structure viable pathways, and engage with stakeholders in a way that aligns with real-world dynamics.
